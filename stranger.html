Stranger

Stranger is not just a spaceplane; it's a leap into the unknown, a testament to human ingenuity, and a symbol of our unwavering commitment to space exploration. Here's why Stranger is poised to revolutionize the way we navigate the cosmos...

Project Eeloo

At the heart of Stranger lies the groundbreaking J-N500 'Project Eeloo' multimode nuclear engine that redefines propulsion in the world of space travel. This engine offers unmatched efficiency in two operational modes.

Here's what sets it apart:

  1. Airbreathing Mode
    Utilizes the atmosphere as a reaction mass, eliminating the need for additional propellants during the initial stages of ascent. This innovation significantly reduces the cost and environmental impact of space travel.

  2. Closed-Cycle Mode:
    When the air thins out in the upper reaches of the atmosphere, the engine seamlessly transitions to liquid hydrogen as its propellant source, enabling it to continue its journey to the stars.
